Supply Chain Process Map

NOVAEO, LLC · Receiving → Inventory → Discrepancy → Reporting · Living document

How it all fits together

Inventory accuracy is a single loop. Every receipt logged at the dock adds to theoretical inventory; every manufacturing order and shipment deducts from it. Each Monday, physical cycle counts compare that theory against reality. Any gap becomes a tracked discrepancy that must be recounted, root-caused, approved, and adjusted — never silently absorbed. Critical cases (high value, organic, or suspected mix-ups) escalate to incident and organic-integrity controls. Weekly reporting turns closed cases into preventive actions, which feed back into how we receive, store, and count.

Discrepancy

Inventory Discrepancy Tracking SOP · SOP-0001 ▶

Classify by severity, log in the Discrepancy Log, recount within 24 hours, investigate root cause, and adjust only with approval.

flowchart TD
    IN(["Variance detected — 03"]):::term --> VAL["Calculate Diff $ value
units × unit cost (iSKU Master)"]:::proc
    VAL --> SEV{"Classify severity"}:::dec
    SEV -->|"Within tolerance
≤2% AND ≤$50"| TA["Log + adjust
no investigation"]:::proc
    TA --> CL(["Close row"]):::term
    SEV -->|"Low / Medium / High"| LOG["Log in Discrepancy Log
DISC-MMDDYY-##"]:::proc
    SEV -->|"Critical: >$2,500 · organic
· suspected mix-up"| CR["Log + → 05 Escalation"]:::crit
    LOG --> RC["Recount within 24 h
second person · adjacent bins

    RC --> RM{"Recount matches

    RM -->|Yes| CLT["Close as Data Entry/Timing

    ADJ --> CL
    CL --> RPT(["→ 06 Report & Trend"]):::term
