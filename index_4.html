Supply Chain Process Map

NOVAEO, LLC · Sourcing → Receiving → Inventory → Production → QA → Fulfillment → Customer Service · Living document

How it all fits together

The supply chain is one continuous loop. Sourcing places verified purchase orders; every receipt logged at the dock adds to theoretical inventory; staging and production consume it against BPRs and add finished goods back; QA and organic controls gate every step; fulfillment deducts what ships; and customer service in Gorgias closes the loop — complaints and returns feed investigations, incidents, and the weekly report, whose preventive actions flow back into how we source, receive, count, and produce. Nothing is silently absorbed: every variance, hold, complaint, and return has an owner, a log, and a closure path. The MOFO Board (Baserow), displayed on the warehouse screen, shows each day’s pending MOs and FOs; marking an order COMPLETED in the app is what closes it out of the next day’s pending list.

Production & Manufacturing

BPR per MMR · End of Production Closeout ▶

The general manufacturing flow, common to every product: the day starts from the MOFO Board on the warehouse screen, each batch runs against a Batch Production Record that reproduces the product's Master Manufacturing Record with initials and verification at every step, and the day closes with a formal closeout and the MO marked COMPLETED in the board.
